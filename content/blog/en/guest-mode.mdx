The saddest statistic in mobile: 77% of users abandon an app within three days of installing it. The number-one reason: being asked to register before seeing value.

## What a guest can do

- See every place in the catalog.
- Filter by category.
- Open maps and details.
- Read the journal and events.
- Check the weather.

## What requires an account

- Save synced favorites.
- Generate AI routes (quota controlled).
- Check in at places.
- Leave reviews.

## The transition

When a guest tries a gated action, the app doesn't wall them off — it gives context. "This feature needs an account. Your current favorites are preserved." And the signup flow takes one screen, not three.

We convert 31% of active guests. Not a feat — that's what happens when you let people decide when to trust you.
